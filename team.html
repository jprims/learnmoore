Jennifer Logg

PhD Candidate

I am a PhD candidate in the University of California, Berkeley's Haas Management Group. My research interests include judgment and decision making. My dissertation tests why and when people trust advice from algorithms over other people. My other work focuses on overconfidence and how people make estimates and predictions about the world around them. Prior to graduate school, I conducted research at Columbia University on decision making and cooperative behavior.
